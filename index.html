Welcome to QR Code Generator

This application allows you to generate QR codes easily. You can input a URL or key-value pairs, and the app will generate a scannable QR code for you.

Features include:

  • Simple and easy-to-use interface
  • Supports both URLs and key-value inputs
  • Customizable QR code generation
  • Download and save QR codes to your preferred location
